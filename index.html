GT Studio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

GT Studio is a business management app for small 3D printing studios and makers. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

GT Studio does not sell personal data and does not use advertising SDKs.

Information The App Handles

GT Studio may store information that you or your team enter into the app, including:

  • Local staff account names and login details.
  • Product names, SKUs, prices, estimated costs, and production details.
  • Material, color, stock, inventory movement, waste, and supplier notes.
  • Order details, customer names, customer notes, and customer contact numbers if you enter them.
  • Delivery company names, delivery state, settlement state, return state, and related notes.
  • Payment records, including payment status, method, amount, reference, and notes.
  • Business settings such as currency, numbering rules, and profit targets.

Local Storage

Most GT Studio data is stored locally on your device using a local database. This data is used to provide app features such as orders, catalog management, inventory tracking, payment tracking, delivery review, team management, and analytics.

Optional Workspace Sync

If you choose to connect a shared workspace or sign in with a Google account, GT Studio may use that connection to sync the studio data you enter. This can help you restore, share, or manage your studio records across your own workflow.

Google's handling of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

How Information Is Used

Information in GT Studio is used only to support the app's business workflow, including creating orders, managing products, tracking stock, recording payments, reviewing delivery companies, calculating profit, showing alerts, managing local users, and syncing or restoring data when enabled.

Sharing Of Information

GT Studio does not sell personal data. Information may be shared only when you choose to connect a shared workspace or account, when it is required to provide app functionality you requested, or when required by law.

Data Security

GT Studio relies on the security protections provided by your Android device and, if enabled, the connected workspace provider. You are responsible for protecting access to your device, app login, Google account, and any workspace connected to the app.

Data Retention And Deletion

Data remains in the app until you delete it, clear the app's local data, uninstall the app, or remove it from any connected workspace. If workspace sync is enabled, deleting local app data may not automatically delete data stored in your connected workspace.

To request help with data deletion or privacy questions, contact us using the email below.

Children's Privacy

GT Studio is intended for business and studio management use. It is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
